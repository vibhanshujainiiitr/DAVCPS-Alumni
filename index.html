    About Us

    The Alumni Association of D.A.V. Centenary Public School Ajmer is the group of students who have completed their studies from the campus. All the students are brighter in studies along with good confidence in co-curricular activities. And this is the gift to the institute from its Alumni Group
    Also, note that this is a self-functioning organization and there is no connection between the institute and us at present.
